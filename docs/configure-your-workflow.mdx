# Configure Your Workflow

### **1.** Set Up the REST Service Trigger

1. Click on the **Select Trigger** in the Canvas area.\
   The **Select a Trigger** modal appears.
2. Select **RESTService** and click **Save**.\
   The **RESTService** trigger is added to the workflow.
3. In the **REST Service Configuration** panel, click on the **Configure It Yourself** menu card.\
   The RESTService Configuration menu appears.
4. Fill in the required details in the provided fields, such as the **Path**.
5. Click **Save** to apply the configuration.

### **2.** Configure the Response Activity

1. Navigate to the **Activity** tab on the left sidebar menu.
2. Expand the **Common** folder.
3. Drag and drop the **Response** activity onto the **Canvas** area.
4. Click on the **Input** tab of the **Response** activity to configure it.
5. In the **Target** panel, hover over the **body** object and click on the **ellipsis (â‹¯)**.
6. A context menu appears. Click **Expand**.\
   The **Add a Schema** modal appears.
7. Click **Search Registry** and search for the **"message"** schema.
8. Select **message** and click **Import** to import the schema.
9. In the **Input** tab Expand the **body** object.
10. In the **Mapping Expression** of the **message** node, enter a simple message, such as `"Hello World"`.
11. Click **Save** to apply the configuration.
